Welcome to Bamboo Panda

Bamboo Panda is your newest destination for quick and delicious Chinese cuisine. Located in Belgrade, serving up mouthwatering dishes for pick-up and delivery through Wolt.

Who We Are

We're a team of food enthusiasts passionate about bringing the flavors of China straight to your doorstep. From classic favorites to innovative creations, our menu offers a variety of options to satisfy your cravings.

What Sets Us Apart

At Bamboo Panda, we're all about convenience and flavor. With no seating area, our focus is on fast service and top-quality takeout. Whether you're ordering for yourself or a group, we're here to make sure your meal is fresh, hot, and delicious every time.

Our Commitment

We're committed to using the freshest ingredients and authentic recipes to deliver an authentic Chinese dining experience, even if you're enjoying it in the comfort of your own home.
